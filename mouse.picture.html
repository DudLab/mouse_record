mouse.picture module¶

Overview¶

The mouse-picture program takes a photo of the background, and saves it into the user-specified directory. The generated photos are time-stamped and in JPEG format.

API¶

mouse.picture.main(*argv)[source]¶

Takes a photo and saves it to a user-specified directory.

Parameters:argv (str) – Arguments are stored as a list

Notes

These are the parameters of the arguments.

  • folder(str): File directory
